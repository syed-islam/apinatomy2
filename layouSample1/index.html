Instructions:

Click to add new node
Click and drag from one node to another to create link
Click on link to select link
Click on node to select node
Press Delete or Backspace to remove selected link or node.
Press 'l', 'r' , 'b' to change direction of selected link.
Press 'f' to make a slected node sticky. Sticky nodes are reflected using black border.
Press and hold 'd' and click and drag on a node of move it
